Engagement Models

Operational Leadership, On Demand

Four engagement models designed to meet you where you are — from pre-opening builds to operational turnarounds and fractional executive support.

01

Fractional F&B Leadership

For restaurants and hotel groups that need a veteran operator 2–4 days per week. Stabilize operations, build systems, and develop your team without the overhead of a full-time executive hire.

  • Weekly on-site operational presence
  • P&L monitoring and cost control
  • Team training and performance management
  • Vendor and supplier management
02

Pre-Opening & New Concept Builds

From construction walkthroughs to opening night — build the team, write SOPs, coordinate vendors, and create an operating backbone before doors open.

  • Hiring and onboarding full FOH & BOH teams
  • SOP and training manual development
  • Vendor and supply chain setup
  • Soft opening and opening-night execution
03

Operations Recovery & Restructuring

When labor costs drift, service standards drop, or turnover hurts consistency — diagnose quickly and install systems that stick.

  • On-site operational diagnostic (3–5 days)
  • Labor cost and scheduling audit
  • Service standards rebuild
  • Team accountability systems
04

Banquet & Catering Operations

High-volume event leadership for hotels, venues, and catering groups across South Florida. Pre-opening setup, staffing models, and execution systems.

  • Event staffing models and scheduling
  • Catering SOP and BEO workflow development
  • Pre-opening banquet department build-out
  • On-site event execution leadership
